Insights

The exploration through the project has brought us on new understandings and provided valuable insights.

Proposed improvement of the contend-based and collaborative filter based on findings:

Our content based and collaborative filtering experience model are quite straightforward and intuitive. However, the limit of our programs are quite significant that they can only handle limited scope of the category. Since our tools rely on static keyword matching, they cannot digest nuanced and context-dependent quareis like "Dark, Rainy Mood of Jazz". Therefore, I came up with a suggested evolutionary roadmap of the program.

I believe that we can develop our model by implementing techniques based on our findings. The implementation plan would be introducing semantic embedding, RAG pipeline and contextual re-ranking by order.

Through embedding, we can convert the metadata into a high dimentional vector and store them on the database. By implementing a Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipeline, user queries interact with a Vector Database to ground recommendations in real-time context. Moreover, the contextual re-ranking can update the model continuously via reflecting real-time user data. To ensure the validity and stability of our program, we can conduct a further study regarding backend, vector database, and AI intelligence.

By developing our model into a more sophisticated version, we anticipate the improvement of intend-driven discovery and user credibility. Beyond matching keywords, the system will be capable of interpreting the complex user requirements and satisfing user by detailed suggestion.
